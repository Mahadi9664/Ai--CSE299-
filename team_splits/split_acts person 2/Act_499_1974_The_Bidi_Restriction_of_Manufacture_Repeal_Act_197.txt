================================================================================
ACT INDEX: 499
ACT TITLE: The Bidi (Restriction of Manufacture) (Repeal) Act, 1974
ACT NO: IX
ACT YEAR: 1974
================================================================================

GOVERNMENT CONTEXT:
  - Government System: Parliamentary Democracy
  - Head of Government: Sheikh Mujibur Rahman
  - Designation: Prime Minister of Bangladesh
  - Period: 1973-1975

LEGAL SYSTEM CONTEXT:
  - Period: Early Independent Bangladesh (1971-1975)
  - Primary Laws: Laws Continuance Enforcement Order 1971, Constitution of Bangladesh 1972
  - Court System: Supreme Court with High Court and Appellate Divisions, Subordinate Courts

SECTIONS:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Section 1:
1. (1) This Act may be called theBidi (Restriction of Manufacture) (Repeal) Act, 1974.(2) It shall come into force at once.


Section 2:
2. The Bidi (Restriction of Manufacture) Ordinance, 1966 (E.P. Ord. IV of 1966) is hereby repealed.
