================================================================================
ACT INDEX: 424
ACT TITLE: The East Pakistan Madrasah Education Ordinance (Repeal) Order, 1972 (President's Order)
ACT NO: 43
ACT YEAR: 1972
================================================================================

GOVERNMENT CONTEXT:
  - Government System: Parliamentary Democracy
  - Head of Government: Sheikh Mujibur Rahman
  - Designation: Prime Minister of Bangladesh
  - Period: 1972-1973

LEGAL SYSTEM CONTEXT:
  - Period: Early Independent Bangladesh (1971-1975)
  - Primary Laws: Laws Continuance Enforcement Order 1971, Constitution of Bangladesh 1972
  - Court System: Supreme Court with High Court and Appellate Divisions, Subordinate Courts

SECTIONS:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Section 1:
1. (1) This Order may be called the East Pakistan Madrasah Education Ordinance (Repeal) Order, 1972.(2) It shall come into force at once.


Section 2:
2. The East Pakistan Madrasah Education Ordinance, 1971, is hereby repealed.
