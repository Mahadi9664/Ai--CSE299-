ACT TITLE: The Bangladesh (Adaptation of University Laws) Ordinance, 1972
ACT NO: I
ACT YEAR: 1972
================================================================================

GOVERNMENT CONTEXT:
  - Government System: Parliamentary Democracy
  - Head of Government: Sheikh Mujibur Rahman
  - Designation: Prime Minister of Bangladesh
  - Period: 1972-1973

LEGAL SYSTEM CONTEXT:
  - Period: Early Independent Bangladesh (1971-1975)
  - Primary Laws: Laws Continuance Enforcement Order 1971, Constitution of Bangladesh 1972
  - Court System: Supreme Court with High Court and Appellate Divisions, Subordinate Courts

SECTIONS:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Section 1:
1. (1) This Ordinance may be called theBangladesh (Adaptation of University Laws) Ordinance, 1972.(2) It shall come into force at once and shall be deemed to have taken effect on the 16th day of December, 1971.


Section 2:
2. (1) As from the 16th day of December, 1971, the university laws mentioned in sub-section (2)  shall, until repealed or altered or amended by the Legislature of Bangladesh or any other competent authority, be subject to the adaptations specified in that sub-section.E.
