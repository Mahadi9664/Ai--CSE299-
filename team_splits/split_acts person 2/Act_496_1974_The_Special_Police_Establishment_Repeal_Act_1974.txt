================================================================================
ACT INDEX: 496
ACT TITLE: The Special Police Establishment (Repeal) Act, 1974
ACT NO: II
ACT YEAR: 1974
================================================================================

GOVERNMENT CONTEXT:
  - Government System: Parliamentary Democracy
  - Head of Government: Sheikh Mujibur Rahman
  - Designation: Prime Minister of Bangladesh
  - Period: 1973-1975

LEGAL SYSTEM CONTEXT:
  - Period: Early Independent Bangladesh (1971-1975)
  - Primary Laws: Laws Continuance Enforcement Order 1971, Constitution of Bangladesh 1972
  - Court System: Supreme Court with High Court and Appellate Divisions, Subordinate Courts

SECTIONS:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Section 1:
1. (1) This Act may be called theSpecial Police Establishment (Repeal) Act, 1974.(2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the 17th day of October, 1973.


Section 2:
2. The Special Police Establishment Ordinance, 1948 (VIII of 1948), is hereby repealed.


Section 3:
3. The Special Police Establishment (Repeal) Ordinance, 1973 (XX of 1973), is hereby repealed.
