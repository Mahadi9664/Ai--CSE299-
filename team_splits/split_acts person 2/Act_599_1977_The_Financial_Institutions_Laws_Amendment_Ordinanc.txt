================================================================================
ACT INDEX: 599
ACT TITLE: The Financial Institutions Laws Amendment Ordinance, 1977
ACT NO: LXI
ACT YEAR: 1977
================================================================================

GOVERNMENT CONTEXT:
  - Government System: Military Rule transitioning to Democracy
  - Head of Government: Ziaur Rahman
  - Designation: President of Bangladesh
  - Period: 1977-1981

LEGAL SYSTEM CONTEXT:
  - Period: Military Rule Era (1975-1990)
  - Primary Laws: Martial Law Proclamations, 5th Amendment (1979), 7th Amendment (1986)
  - Court System: Supreme Court (limited powers), Martial Law Courts, Civil Courts

SECTIONS:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Section 1:
1. This Ordinance may be called theFinancial Institutions Laws Amendment Ordinance, 1977.


Section 2:
2. The laws mentioned in column I of the Schedule shall be amended in the manner indicated in column II thereof.


Section 3:
3. Any appointment of a General Manager in the Sangstha, Bank or Corporation established by or under a law mentioned in column I of the Schedule made by the Government at any time before the promulgation of this Ordinance shall be deemed to have been made under the relevant provision of such law, as amended by this Ordinance, and every such appointment shall have effect accordingly.
