================================================================================
ACT INDEX: 517
ACT TITLE: The Primary Education Laws (Repeal) Act, 1974
ACT NO: LV
ACT YEAR: 1974
================================================================================

GOVERNMENT CONTEXT:
  - Government System: Parliamentary Democracy
  - Head of Government: Sheikh Mujibur Rahman
  - Designation: Prime Minister of Bangladesh
  - Period: 1973-1975

LEGAL SYSTEM CONTEXT:
  - Period: Early Independent Bangladesh (1971-1975)
  - Primary Laws: Laws Continuance Enforcement Order 1971, Constitution of Bangladesh 1972
  - Court System: Supreme Court with High Court and Appellate Divisions, Subordinate Courts

SECTIONS:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Section 1:
1. This Act may be called thePrimary Education Laws (Repeal) Act, 1974.


Section 2:
2. The Primary Education Act, 1919 (Ben. Act IV of 1919), the Rural Primary Education Act, 1930 (Ben. Act VII of 1930), and the Rural Primary Education (Supplementary Provisions) Act, 1957 (E.P. Act XXVIII of 1957), are hereby repealed.
