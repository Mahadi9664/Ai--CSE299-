================================================================================
ACT INDEX: 433
ACT TITLE: The Bangladesh Taxation Laws (Adaptation) Order, 1972 (President's Order)
ACT NO: 62
ACT YEAR: 1972
================================================================================

GOVERNMENT CONTEXT:
  - Government System: Parliamentary Democracy
  - Head of Government: Sheikh Mujibur Rahman
  - Designation: Prime Minister of Bangladesh
  - Period: 1972-1973

LEGAL SYSTEM CONTEXT:
  - Period: Early Independent Bangladesh (1971-1975)
  - Primary Laws: Laws Continuance Enforcement Order 1971, Constitution of Bangladesh 1972
  - Court System: Supreme Court with High Court and Appellate Divisions, Subordinate Courts

SECTIONS:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Section 1:
1. (1) This Order may be called the Bangladesh Taxation Laws (Adaptation) Order, 1972.(2) It shall come into force at once and shall be deemed to have taken effect on the 26th day of March, 1971.


Section 2:
2. The taxation laws specified in the Schedule shall, until repealed or amended by competent authority, have effect and be deemed to have at all material times had effect subject to the modifications directed by the Schedule.
