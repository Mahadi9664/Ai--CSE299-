ACT INDEX: 719
ACT TITLE: The Ghousul Azam Abdul Qader Jillani Mosque Trust (Repeal) Ordinance, 1985
ACT NO: XVII
ACT YEAR: 1985
================================================================================

GOVERNMENT CONTEXT:
  - Government System: Military Rule
  - Head of Government: Lt. Gen. Hussain Muhammad Ershad
  - Designation: President of Bangladesh (Military ruler)
  - Period: 1982-1990

LEGAL SYSTEM CONTEXT:
  - Period: Military Rule Era (1975-1990)
  - Primary Laws: Martial Law Proclamations, 5th Amendment (1979), 7th Amendment (1986)
  - Court System: Supreme Court (limited powers), Martial Law Courts, Civil Courts

SECTIONS:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Section 1:
1. This Ordinance may be called theGhousul Azam Abdul Qader Jillani Mosque Trust (Repeal) Ordinance, 1985.


Section 2:
2. The Ghousul Azam Abdul Qader Jillani Mosque Trust Ordinance, 1984 (XLVI of 1984), is hereby repealed.
