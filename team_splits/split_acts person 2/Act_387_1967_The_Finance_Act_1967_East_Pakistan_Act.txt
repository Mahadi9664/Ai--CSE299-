Section 1:
1. (1) This Act may be called the1[* * *] Finance Act, 1967.(2) It extends to the whole of2[Bangladesh].(3) It shall come into force on the first day of July, 1967.


Section 2:
2. (1) There shall be levied and collected by the3[Bangladesh] an additional Development and relief tax from every Malik or tenant at the rate of fifteen per cent of land revenue or rent payable by him in respect of the land comprised in his holding or tenancy with effect from the 1st day of Baishakh, 1377.(2) The additional development and relief tax levied under sub-section (1) shall be realised in the manner in which rent is realised by the4[Bangladesh].

Section 11:
11. If a person, on whom a tax is levied or who is responsible for the collection and payment of any tax or toll under this Act fails to pay the tax or toll or fails to collect and pay the tax or the toll as provided in this Act and the rules made thereunder, he shall be liable to a penalty not exceeding the amount of the tax or toll payable.


Section 12:
12. A tax or toll leviable under any provision of this Act or any penalty imposed thereunder shall be recoverable as a public demand under the5[* * *] Public Demands Recovery Act, 1913.


Section 13:
13. No suit shall lie in any Civil Court to set aside or modify any assessment of tax or toll made under this Act and the rules made thereunder.


Section 14:
14. No suit, prosecution or legal proceeding shall lie against any person for anything in good faith done or intended to be done under this Act or the rules made thereunder.
