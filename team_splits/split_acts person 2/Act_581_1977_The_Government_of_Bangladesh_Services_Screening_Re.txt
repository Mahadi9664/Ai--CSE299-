================================================================================
ACT INDEX: 581
ACT TITLE: The Government of Bangladesh (Services Screening) (Repeal) Ordinance, 1977
ACT NO: XV
ACT YEAR: 1977
================================================================================

GOVERNMENT CONTEXT:
  - Government System: Military Rule transitioning to Democracy
  - Head of Government: Ziaur Rahman
  - Designation: President of Bangladesh
  - Period: 1977-1981

LEGAL SYSTEM CONTEXT:
  - Period: Military Rule Era (1975-1990)
  - Primary Laws: Martial Law Proclamations, 5th Amendment (1979), 7th Amendment (1986)
  - Court System: Supreme Court (limited powers), Martial Law Courts, Civil Courts

SECTIONS:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Section 1:
1. This Ordinance may be called theGovernment of Bangladesh (Services Screening) (Repeal) Ordinance, 1977.


Section 2:
2. The Government of Bangladesh (Services Screening) Order, 1972 (P.O. No. 67 of 1972), is hereby repealed.


Section 3:
3. Notwithstanding the repeal of the Government of Bangladesh (Services Screening) Order, 1972 (P.O. No. 67 of 1972) by section 2, the Government may, at any time, either of its own motion or on application, review any order passed by it under clause (2) of Article 6 of that Order.
